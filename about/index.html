About

[row cols_nr=”2″][col size=”6″]

I’m a travelling and fashion photographer who currently resides in New York City. I grew up in Staffordshire, England where I discovered my interest in photography at an early age. Later moved to London to pursue fashion photography full-time before making NYC my home in 2015.

I have won a boatload of awards for my work, and I’m grateful for every single one of them, but I’ve always been unsure of whether I earned them or whether the jury was rigged.  Let’s be friends. Better yet, let’s swim the English Channel.

                    

What’s your story?

I’d love to help you tell it. Let’s shoot some good stuff together. Let’s be friends. Better yet, let’s swim the English Channel.
